PARIS


HISTORY
Origins and Construction:

The Eiffel Tower was built for the 1889 World's Fair in Paris to commemorate the centenary of the French Revolution. It was initially intended to be a temporary structure but became a beloved symbol of Paris and France. Its construction was a feat of engineering, completed in just over two years using revolutionary techniques.

Architecture
Architectural Mastery

Eiffel Tower's Construction began on the Champ de Mars. The tower was completed and inaugurated in 1889, becoming the tallest structure in the world at the time. The tower was built in just over two years, using 18,000 metal pieces and 2.5 million rivets.

Tourist
Tourist Attraction

The tower became a major tourist destination, with millions visiting each year.The Eiffel Tower is now an iconic symbol of Paris and France, representing engineering prowess and artistic expression.
